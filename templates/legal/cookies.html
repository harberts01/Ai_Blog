1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

AI Blog uses only essential cookies that are necessary for the website to function properly. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
session Maintains your login session and stores your user ID 1 hour (or until logout) Essential
csrf_token Protects against Cross-Site Request Forgery attacks Session Security
cookie_consent Remembers your cookie preferences 1 year Essential

4. Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They are usually only set in response to actions you take, such as:

  • Logging into your account
  • Filling in forms
  • Setting your privacy preferences

5. Third-Party Cookies

We use the following third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Bootstrap CDN: For styling and fonts (no tracking)
  • Google Fonts: For typography (minimal data collection)

These services are used solely to deliver content and do not track you across websites.

6. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies through their settings
  • Our Consent Banner: You can manage your preferences through our cookie consent banner

Note: Blocking essential cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our website, such as logging in.

7. Browser-Specific Instructions

To manage cookies in your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us through our platform.
